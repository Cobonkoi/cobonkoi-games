Hard Brexit

The news of Brexit is everywhere at the moment. Mostly about how badly it's been going. In this game you get to take control of Theresa May and try to make a Brexit plan that gets accepted. You'll need to balance quickly scribbling down your policies, with using that Theresa May Charm to keep your politicians happy, and to stop them from walking out on you.

Sounds easy right? But wait, there's more!

While doing all of the above, you'll also have to protect your politicians from the the evil Left wing who will do anything to snatch them away to the dark side.
