About Cobonkoi Games

Cobonkoi Games is an Indie Games Development company created by two friends with a common interest, creating great player experiences. We are located in Greater Manchester in the UK and are working from our homes, using Gamemaker Studio 2. Connor is the Artist and soung guy. Daniel the programmer. You can read more about us below.
